Luke Moroney

Writer

Luke Moroney is a poetry and short-fiction writer living in Naarm (Melbourne), Australia. He has previously appeared in Antithesis Journal (University of Melbourne), and Poetic Portraits anthology (RMIT). Luke is currently in his final year of a Bachelor of Arts (Creative Writing) at RMIT. His work engages with the concept of embodiment through place-based methods and ideas of human/ non-human co-habitation.
